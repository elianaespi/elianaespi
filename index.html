About Me

My name is Eliana Espinosa, I am currently a student at St. Thomas University, majoring in Mathematics.I am 21 years old, I’m a cancer survivor, and I was born in Brooklyn, NY but was raised in Miami, FL. As a Math major, i have worked on several projects with R programming language and you can see them by clicking on the Projects tab. When I am not working or studying I like to write lyrics about different experiences in life. I have been writing for about 3 years and my best song ideas tend to come to me late at night when I am trying to fall asleep.
